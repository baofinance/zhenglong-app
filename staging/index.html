Use Cases

Zhenglong Protocol empowers innovation by tokenizing any reliable data source into yield-generating markets. If there's reliable price data and market demand, it can be tokenized.

ETH Gas Price Tokens
Hedge transaction fee exposure or speculate on Ethereum congestion.
zheGAS is pegged to Ethereum gas prices, letting users hedge or speculate on network fees.
Governance Power Markets
Track and gain exposure to governance token yields without direct protocol staking.
Tokens track yields from protocol governance (e.g. Convex, Aura) including bribes and rewards, enabling speculation on governance value without lock-ups.
Crypto Yield Markets
Track and gain exposure to DeFi yield rates without direct protocol interaction.
zheYIELD tracks liquid staking yields while zheLEND follows lending market rates, enabling yield speculation and hedging without protocol lock-ins.
Crypto Adoption Metrics
Speculate on crypto adoption growth without investing in a single asset.
zheADOPT tracks blockchain adoption or transaction metrics, enabling new ways to bet on crypto's future.
Currency Markets
Global, decentralized FX markets accessible 24/7 for both major and emerging market currencies.
Tokens like zheEUR, zheJPY (major) and zheINR, zheBRL (emerging) enable on-chain trading and hedging of global currencies, expanding access to forex markets through DeFi.
Precious Metals
Store of value alternatives or hedging instruments fully backed by crypto collateral.
zheGOLD and zheSILVER provide synthetic, on-chain exposure to gold and silver prices.
Energy Commodities
Traders or companies hedge energy exposure directly through DeFi markets.
zheOIL and zheGAS track oil and natural gas prices, bringing energy markets to DeFi.
Carbon Credit & ESG Tokens
Businesses offset carbon exposure directly via DeFi, incentivizing green initiatives.
zheCO2 tracks global carbon prices, allowing companies to offset emissions and participate in ESG markets on-chain.
Equity Indices
DeFi traders gain exposure to equities without leaving crypto ecosystems.
Synthetic tokens like zheSPX and zheNDX track S&P 500 and Nasdaq indices, enabling global, 24/7 access to equity markets.
Individual Stock Tokens
Global accessibility to popular stocks, especially for users in restricted jurisdictions.
zheTSLA and zheAAPL provide synthetic exposure to Tesla and Apple, democratizing access to top equities.
Weather Derivatives
Farmers hedge against drought or excessive rainfall; insurers offer coverage without centralized providers.
Pegged to rainfall or weather indices, zheRAIN enables decentralized weather risk management for agriculture and insurance markets.
Athlete Performance Metrics
Fans gain financial exposure to athlete performance, boosting engagement.
zheGOALS is pegged to athlete stats, letting fans and traders speculate on or hedge against sports performance.
Music & Streaming Revenue
Artists tokenize future royalties, allowing direct fan investment.
zheSTREAM tracks streaming revenue indexes or royalties, opening new funding models for creators.
Inflation & CPI Tokens
Users hedge against inflation risk directly on-chain, protecting their purchasing power.
zheCPI is pegged to consumer price indices, enabling on-chain inflation hedging for DeFi users.
GDP Growth Tokens
Economists or investors hedge against or speculate on economic growth data.
zheGDP tracks GDP growth, allowing macro speculation and risk management on-chain.
AI & Machine Learning Performance
Investors gain exposure to AI advancements, rewarding R&D success transparently.
zheAI is pegged to AI performance benchmarks, letting users invest in the growth of AI technology.
